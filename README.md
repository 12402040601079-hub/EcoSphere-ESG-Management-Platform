# EcoSphere ESG Management Platform

## Overview
EcoSphere is a comprehensive platform designed to integrate Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) tracking directly into day-to-day operations. It enables organizations to measure, manage, and improve their sustainability metrics while encouraging active employee participation through gamification.

Instead of manual spreadsheet reporting, EcoSphere bridges the gap between raw operational data and executive dashboards by automatically crunching carbon numbers, tracking CSR involvement, and enforcing policy compliance.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Environmental (Carbon Tracking)
Tracks carbon emissions from daily business operations (e.g., fleet travel, electricity usage, waste). 
- **Emission Factors:** Admins configure standard multipliers (e.g., 0.27 kg CO₂ per km of Diesel).
- **Carbon Transactions:** Employees log raw activities, and the system automatically calculates the carbon footprint based on the emission factors.
- **Environmental Goals:** Managers set targets (e.g., "Reduce Scope 1 emissions by 20%").

### 2. Social (CSR & Engagement)
Manages Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives.
- **CSR Activities:** Managers create events like "Tree Planting Drives".
- **Employee Participation:** Employees log their contributed hours and upload proof. Managers review and approve these submissions to drive the department's Social Score.

### 3. Governance (Policies & Compliance)
Ensures the organization meets regulatory and internal standards.
- **ESG Policies:** HR uploads policies that employees must digitally acknowledge.
- **Compliance Issues:** Auditors or Managers can raise open issues with assigned owners, severity levels, and strict due dates.

### 4. Gamification & Rewards
Drives user adoption by turning sustainability into a rewarding experience.
- **Challenges:** Time-bound goals (e.g., "Zero Waste July") that employees can participate in.
- **XP & Badges:** Employees earn Experience Points (XP) for positive actions. The system automatically awards badges based on flexible JSON rules (e.g., "Awarded for 500+ XP").
- **Rewards:** Employees can spend their points in a company catalog (e.g., Gift Cards).

## Technical Architecture

The platform is designed with a strict separation of concerns, utilizing an event-driven micro-architecture to ensure fast dashboard load times.

- **Backend Framework:** NestJS (TypeScript)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Strict relational integrity using ENUMs, UUIDs, and CHECK constraints)
- **ORM:** Prisma
- **Caching & Background Jobs:** Redis & BullMQ (Used for heavy math like calculating Department ESG Scores and auto-awarding badges without blocking the main API thread).

---

## Database Design & Project Structure

The database strictly isolates data into three logical tiers to maintain data integrity. The schema definitions can be found in the `/database` folder (`schema.sql` and `schema.prisma`).

### 1. Master Configuration Data
These tables are configured once by **Admins** and dictate the rules of the system.
- `Organization`, `Department`, `User`
- `EmissionFactor` (Multipliers for carbon math)
- `ESGConfiguration` (Weights defining how much Env/Soc/Gov affect the final score)
- `ESGPolicy`, `Badge`, `Reward`, `Category`

### 2. Transactional Data
The daily flow of data generated by **Employees** and approved by **Managers**.
- `CarbonTransaction`, `CSRActivity`, `EmployeeParticipation`
- `PolicyAcknowledgement`, `ComplianceIssue`
- `Challenge`, `ChallengeParticipation`, `EnvironmentalGoal`

### 3. System-Generated Data
Data created entirely by **Background Jobs**. Humans never manually edit these tables.
- `DepartmentScore` (The final calculated ESG score cached for quick dashboard reads)
- `UserBadge` (Auto-awarded when XP thresholds are met)
- `RewardRedemption` (Inventory deduction logs)
- `Notification`, `AuditLog` (Immutable security logs)
